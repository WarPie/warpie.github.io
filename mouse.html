Mouse¶

The Mouse file holds functions and procedures that are related to mouse-stuffz.

Moving the mouse¶

Move the mouse to a x, y coord.
  • Mouse.Move(100, 100);
Move the mouse to a point.
  • Mouse.Move(Point(100, 100));

Move the mouse to a random (gauss) point in a box. - Mouse.Move(IntToBox(0, 0, 100, 100));

Move the mouse to a random (gauss) point in a circle. - Mouse.Move(Point(100, 100), 50);

Clicking the mouse¶

Vaild click types: mouse_Left, mouse_Right, mouse_Middle.

Click the mouse at the current position.
  • Mouse.Click(mouse_Left);
Move and click the mouse to a x, y coord.
  • Mouse.Click(100, 100, mouse_Left);
Move and click the mouse to a point.
  • Mouse.Click(Point(100, 100), mouse_Right);

Move and click the mouse to a random (gauss) point in a box. - Mouse.Click(IntToBox(0, 0, 100, 100), mouse_LEft);

Move and click the mouse to a random (gauss) point in a circle. - Mouse.Click(Point(100, 100), 50, mouse_Right);
